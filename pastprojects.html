The Eternal Crown

Class Project

Jan 2023 - April 2023

Artist, programmer and debugger.
A simple RPG created with a small group of people in my Game Dev & Algorithmic Problem Solving class.
The game was hardcoded in C# using MonoGame. I focused primarily on debugging, fixing & cleaning code,
as well as creating all the assets and figuring out how to implement them into the game.

You play as an adventurer whose goal is to travel through a forest in order to find "The Eternal Crown."
This crown is promised to give eternal life, and it's your goal to obtain it by whatever means necessary.

Simple Bug Simulation

Solo Class Project

Nov 19, 2023 - Dec 11, 2023

The second project I've created in Unity, being a simulation of critters roaming an environment.
Each insect has a variety of states such as: Hunting, eating, fleeing, grouping and roaming.
All of which are affected by the creatures surrounding it. They also avoid obstacles as well as walls.

You watch a lively environment full of insects, being able to place as many blue critters as you desire.
They hunt the never-dying flock of bat-bugs, and are eaten by the ferocious red one.

Pokemon Team Generator

Solo Class Project

Nov 15, 2023 - Nov 29, 2023

I was tasked to use one of a select few of available APIs to create a website based on it.
I chose Pokemon as it is a loved series of mine. The website is a team builder which allows
the user to create either completely random or pre-determined teams based off its search functions.

The only thing I would change about this website now that it's "completed" is to add the ability to
save formed teams. Currently only the user's settings are locally stored, however implementing a
team saving option should not be too hard to implement.
