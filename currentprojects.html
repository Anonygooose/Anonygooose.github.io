Dragonfly 44

Personal Passion Project

2018 - Present

This concept is still a MAJOR work in progress, so any ideas or details may change completely!
Still debating between a 2D/2.5D style, it will be a detective psychological-horror game.
Dragonfly 44 takes place in the galaxy it's named after, being a dark-matter twin of the Milky Way.
You are a detective working for an intergalactic agency, trying to solve cases for beings across
the universe. On your first solo case, you discover it's not as simple as it was written out to be,
and end up falling deeper and deeper into a larger situation found within Dragonfly 44.
