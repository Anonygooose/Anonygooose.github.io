Audio Visualizer

This is an audio visualizer created to follow the beats and rhythms of the songs that are playing! The theme is heavily inspired by the game I have been working on for several years now (no information on it yet, though).

What is it?

This is an audio visualizer made for a class project, and customized completely for fun. Multiple aspects of this visualizer relate to the game I am working on, as it's currently my biggest inspiration for creating.

Release

Check out the website for yourself! Be wary of the bright colors and flashing imagery!

What I Gained

Some notable aspects of the current version of this website:
- Utilizing Audio Context to be able to play and adjust music, based off a multitude of settings.
- A loop that actively plays a variety animations, such as the tangerine spinning.

- Hue-shifting the entire project as it loops.

Polska Chata

June 2024
(HTML, CSS, JS)

Polska Chata LLC is a family owned restaurant in Rochester, NY. The owner, Izabela Tarlowska, and her husband & co-owner Janusz Tarlowski were both born and raised in Poland. They aim to achieve an authentic Polish experience by sharing their food and culture with the community.

What is it?

Solo project, tasked to create a functional website for a relative's restaurant. This was a great learning experience for me, as I learned many interesting and important aspects of website creation. It frequently updates as images are provided for each menu item, and I have other planned additions to make the site even more functional for the owners.

What I Gained

Some notable aspects of this site:
- A carousel of different images on the home page
- Navbar changes into a burger menu on smaller screens
- A custom loading screen which waits for everything to loads before displaying it to the user
- Complete responsiveness

Crowd Comic

May 2024 - August 2024
(HTML, CSS, TypeScript, Bootstrap)

Crowd Comic is a collaborative art project created by a community of readers, writers, and artists. Users will help to create a branching narrative greater than the sum of its parts. We are looking to capture the beautiful disharmony that comes from individual visions for how a story unfolds.

Read

Read an ever growing library of user created comics. Reached the end and looking for more? Go back and explore multiple paths to see alternative takes on where the narrative goes.

Create

Feeling inspired? Participate in the collaborative storytelling of Crowd Comic with our drawing experience! Use a variety of tools to create three comic panels and advance your favorite story lines for the rest of the community to see.

Release

Coming soon!

What I Gained

This was an internship over the summer of 2024, where I and a small team of people worked together to create the barebones of a website!
My tasks included:
- Asset production for the Hero and text boxes
- Focusing on the CSS of the Home page and Draw page
- Coming up with a variety of different gameplay ideas

 Crowd Comic was the first internship I ever had, and the first time I used my web-design skills in a professional setting. It allowed me to learn TypeScript and Bootstrap, as well as learn how to properly host websites.
